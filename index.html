Hydro Jetting

    The process of cleaning drainage systems with a machine that creates water pressure running through a distribution head that spins in order to clean the entire circumfrance of the pipe.
    We run the highest quality hydro jet machines at KBI & back this process with A 3 YEAR WARRANTY.

Hydro Jetting Services

Storm Sewer

    Usually ccommercial buildings & Parking lots. These systems are very vulnerable to debris clogging and causing flooding issues.
    These system need regular maintenance in the form of Hydro Jetting.

Storm Sewer Services

Seepage Tile

    This is the pipe work around the perimeter of the foundation that leads to the sump pit and releives hydrostatic pressure.
    These pipes should be inspected and cleaned annually.
